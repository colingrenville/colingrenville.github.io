My Background

I was born and raised in Toronto, Ontario. I grew up an athlete, playing basketball competitively since I was 12. I decided not to pursue basketball at the university level, wanting to move on to different things. Needing something to immerse myself in, I took up Web Development. I took an intensive course at Bitmaker, where I spent pretty much every waking moment coding. Since then, Web Development has become one of my biggest passions.

My Approach

I'm a lover of simple, innovative, and interactive design. I don't think it's enough to just have a plain old website for you or your business. Your website must be sleek, intuitive, and powerful. According to research, 94% of a website's first impressions are design related. Let's make your website stand out with a powerful first impression.

My Interests

In my free time I'm an avid basketball player, steak lover, dog petter, coffee drinker, and a little bit of a fashion aficionado. When it's warm out I like to bike, and when it's cold out I opt to stay inside. I have a Boxer Mastiff named Sam, and a Tabby Cat named Miss Bling Bling (I was 10 and really into gangster rap when I named her). If I'm not working on a website, you'll probably catch me at the gym or chilling out with Sam (Miss Bling Bling isn't very friendly).
